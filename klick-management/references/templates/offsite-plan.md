## Planning Checklist

### 6+ weeks before

- [ ] Set goals and objectives (see above)
- [ ] Determine offsite type and team development stage
- [ ] Schedule date, time, and length
- [ ] Start brainstorming the agenda with your team
- [ ] Book space

### 1 month before

- [ ] Order food and beverages
- [ ] Finalize the agenda and assign a DRI to each session
- [ ] Share rough pre-work templates with DRIs

### 1 week before

- [ ] Finalize all materials
- [ ] Run through the agenda and content with DRIs
- [ ] Send the agenda, pre-reads, and any pre-work to the team
- [ ] Check that all meals are scheduled; confirm headcount
- [ ] Confirm venue setup and any materials needed with the offsite space
- [ ] Determine how to enter the space (entrance code, key contact, etc.)
- [ ] Duplicate the session template below for each planned session
- [ ] Duplicate the icebreaker template below for each planned icebreaker

### 1 day before

- [ ] Send a welcome email to the team (venue, how to get there, how to enter, reminder about pre-reads and pre-work)
- [ ] Confirm details with vendors
- [ ] Collect supplies from the office (pens, markers, sticky notes, flip charts, chargers, AV equipment)

### Day of

- [ ] Open with team check-in, then follow the agenda

### After

- [ ] Send notes and action items to assigned DRIs
- [ ] Send a feedback form to all participants; ask them to rate the offsite
- [ ] Track all action items until complete

## Day-of Offsite Structure

*Standard sequence:*
1. Offsite welcome
2. Check-in exercise
3. Icebreaker (if team stage calls for it)
4. Sessions (repeat session block below as needed)
5. Check-out exercise

---

### Check-in

**Facilitator:**
**Time allotted:** 20–30 minutes
**Prompt to use:**

*Suggested prompts:*
- "Share how you're feeling about the offsite and one objective you have for today." *(Model this yourself first — brief, personal, a little vulnerable.)*
- "Rate how present you are for this offsite on a scale of 1–10 and explain your number."

**Manager model (write yours here before the offsite):**
> *e.g., "It's been a heavy week because... My objective for today is... I'm hoping we..."*

---

### Icebreaker *(optional; use for forming/storming teams)*

**Facilitator:**
**Time allotted:** 15–20 minutes
**Activity:**

*Suggested icebreakers:*

- Share your favorite book, movie, or first concert — and why
- Walk your laptop to a meaningful object in your home and explain why it matters to you *(virtual offsites)*

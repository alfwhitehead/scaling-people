## Recruiter Screen

**Dimensions assessed:** [e.g., Functional expertise, Conscientiousness, Intrinsic motivation]
**Interview length:** 30 minutes

### Background and context questions

*Opening talk track (adapt as needed):*
> "I'd love to hear more about your background in [function]. I'll have a few questions along the way, but let's start there."

**Core questions:**
1. [Walk me through your background in [function]. What parts of the [life cycle / work] have you been most involved in?]
2. [What does success look like in your current role? How are you measured?]
3. [How do you interact with the business / your main stakeholders? How do you approach that partnership?]
4. [Optional: What's one thing you'd change about your current process? How would you go about implementing it?]

**Additional questions if time allows:**
- [Is there a project or process improvement you're most proud of recently?]
- [What has been an ambiguous or challenging situation at work, and how did you navigate it?]

### Rubric

| Dimension | Poor | Good | Strong |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Functional expertise] | [No recent relevant experience; no desire to develop in this area] | [Has solid foundational experience; touches the key aspects of the role] | [Has deep, recent experience directly relevant to what this role demands] |
| [Conscientiousness] | [Unclear or unstructured answers; misses follow-ups] | [Organized, meets commitments, demonstrates preparation] | [Proactively structured; evidence of high standards and follow-through] |
| [Intrinsic motivation] | [Career goals tied to title or compensation only; extrinsic drivers dominate] | [Articulates genuine interest in the work; shows growth orientation] | [Demonstrates deep curiosity and self-direction; sets their own development goals] |

| Total score | Decision |
|---|---|
| [11-12] | Strong pass — proceed to onsite |
